Our Story

Watani Tabiee is an online store dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors and traditions of Afghanistan to the world. Nestled in the heart of Afghanistan, the brand's founders have deep roots in the land, valuing nature's purest treasures and a commitment to quality. They source their natural and local Afghan dry fruits and honey directly from their origins, ensuring a connection to the land and the people who cultivate these exquisite goods. Watani Tabiee stands for purity, free from additives or preservatives, preserving the essence of Afghanistan's bounty. It's not just a store but a bridge that supports local communities, traditions, and fair trade practices. When you choose Watani Tabiee, you're not just purchasing products; you're embracing a piece of Afghanistan's culture, delivered to your doorstep, and celebrating the taste of authenticity.

Watani Tabiee is a testament to the passion, authenticity, and commitment to quality, celebrating the natural and local treasures of Afghanistan.
